About The Firm

Right Team. Right Experience.

TSSK & Associates is a Chartered Accountancy firm established by CA. S. Kanagarasu and CA. T. Selvam. The firm is empanelled with the Comptroller & Auditor General of India (C&AG) to conduct audits of Large PSUs.

The firm is recognised as a Category I Audit Firm eligible to conduct large branch statutory audits of PSU Banks, and is also classified as a Category III firm eligible to conduct statutory audits of PSU Banks.

Planning business resources coupled with compliances of law has become a challenge today. The partners of the firm and their associates have sound theoretical knowledge and vast practical experience. By sharing this with team members and clients, the firm ensures effective and timely redress of issues arising on a routine basis or that can arise later.

The core team comprises Chartered Accountants, lawyers, and business advisers with diverse experience in taxation, audit, business consultancy, information technology, corporate laws, and financing.
